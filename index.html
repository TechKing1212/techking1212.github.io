About Us

AMJ & Associates is a firm of Company Secretaries in practice since 2003, located at the NCR Delhi, India. The Firm is having wide and long experience of serving the corporate clients in various Industries all over India and abroad.

⠀

AMJ & Associates provides various types of secretarial, legal and other services to our clients in the area of Corporate Laws, Industrial Laws, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Law, Environmental Laws, Foreign Exchange & Management Laws, Intellectual Property Laws, etc. We also deal in various types of registration, licensing, etc from the government authorities.

⠀

AMJ & Associates is having expertise in areas like Secretarial Audits, Listing Compliances by Listed Companies, IPO, Mergers and Acquisitions, Take Over, Company Law Matters, Joint Ventures, FEMA Compliances etc.

⠀

AMJ & Associates has a dedicated team of professionals including Company secretaries, Chartered Accountants, Lawyers, Intellectual property experts, technical experts, Cost and work accountants and Information technology consultants, with the specific industry expertise, knowledge, resources and commitment required to meet varied requirements of our wide client base.

⠀

We, at AMJ & Associates believe in providing practical advice to our clients, which matches with their unique business requirements. We deliver our professional advice in a transparent manner and of superior quality.
